General Questions

GymBo is a fitness app for iOS that helps you professionally track and optimize your workouts. With intelligent progression suggestions, various training methods (Standard, Superset, Circuit) and over 190 pre-installed exercises, GymBo is the perfect companion for your training.

GymBo is designed for all fitness levels â€“ from beginners to advanced athletes. The app adapts to your experience level and offers personalized recommendations based on your goals (fitness, weight loss, or muscle building).

GymBo is available for iPhone with iOS 17 or later.

Features

GymBo supports three training methods:

  • Standard Training: Classic sequential training with individual exercises
  • Superset Training: Two exercises are performed alternately without rest (A1â†’A2)
  • Circuit Training: Multiple stations are completed in rounds one after another

The Workout Wizard is an intelligent assistant that recommends suitable exercises based on your preferences and creates a complete workout. You choose:

  • Target muscle groups
  • Available equipment
  • Desired training duration
  • Difficulty level

GymBo includes over 190 pre-installed exercises for all muscle groups. You can also create and save your own exercises.

GymBo analyzes your training history and intelligently suggests when to increase weight or reps. The auto-progression uses three strategies in priority order:

1. Deload Detection (Safety first)

After 3 consecutive incomplete workouts or 2 failed workouts with declining performance, GymBo recommends -10% weight for recovery.

2. Linear Weight Progression (Standard)

When the last 2 workouts with the same weight were completed successfully and no failed attempts in the last 3 workouts:

Equipment Beginner Intermediate Advanced
Barbell +5 kg +2.5 kg +1.25 kg
Dumbbell +2 kg +2 kg +2 kg
Machine +5 kg +5 kg +5 kg

3. Rep Progression (for Muscle Building)

With muscle building as your goal: If the same weight has been used successfully for 3+ workouts and reps are under 15, GymBo recommends +2 reps.

Yes, GymBo automatically calculates meaningful warm-up sets based on your working weight and offers various warm-up strategies.
